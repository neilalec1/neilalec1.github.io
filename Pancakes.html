Chocolate Banana Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 100gms plain flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 pint of milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Knobb of butter for frying
  • 50gms cadbury's milk chocolate
  • 50gms dark chocolate
  • Knobb of butter
  • 2-3 tbsps milk
  • 3 bananas sliced

Method

  1. Make the chocolate sauce.
  2. Break the chocolate into a heat-proof bowl.
  3. Add a knob of butter and 3 tbsps of milk.
  4. Melt for 30 seconds in the microwave.
  5. Remove and stir well until smooth and glossy with no lumps.
  6. To make the pancakes, sieve the flour and salt into a bowl.
  7. Break in the egg and pour in half a pint of milk.
  8. Using a balloon whisk, beat the milk, egg and flour until you have a smooth batter.
  9. Heat a knob of butter in a frying pan/crepe pan.
  10. Turn the heat up high and pour in two ladles of batter to make one pancake.
  11. Cook for a few minutes until browned slightly on one side then flip over to cook the other side.
  12. Serve with sliced banana and chocolate sauce poured over the pancake.

Pimp it! (click on me)

You can use tinned pears instead of bananas, or maple syrup instead of chocolate sauce.
