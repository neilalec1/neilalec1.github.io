Sugo al Pomodoro

Prep Time: 20 Minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1x 450gms tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped frozen onions
  • 1 Knorr stockpot (chicken or vegetable)
  • 2 tbsps olive oil
  • 5/6 basil leaves
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a heavy-bottomed saucepan.
  2. Add the onion and fry gently for about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the tinned tomatoes, stock pot and torn basil.
  4. Add a couple of pinches of salt and several grinds of black pepper.
  5. Bring to the boil then cover with lid and simmer on a low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
