Featured Recipe: Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian dish is a favorite among pasta lovers. It's creamy, salty, and savory all at the same time. And the best part? It only takes 20 minutes to make! Here's what you'll need:

  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • 4 oz. pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

To make the dish, simply cook the spaghetti according to package directions. While the pasta is cooking, cook the pancetta or bacon in a large skillet until crispy. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, garlic, salt, and pepper. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the pancetta. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta and stir until the eggs are cooked and the pasta is coated in a creamy sauce. Serve hot and enjoy!
